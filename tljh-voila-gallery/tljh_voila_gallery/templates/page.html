This gallery contains interactive examples illustrating concepts of image processing. The examples are Jupyter notebooks rendered by Voila.

These examples have been created by Guillaume Witz for the course Fundamentals of Digital Image Processing part of the Advanced Microscopy course given at the Microscopy Imaging Center of Bern University.
